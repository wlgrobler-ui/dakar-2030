Stewardship Over Ego

This campaign is grounded in gratitude — for health, family, provision, opportunity, and the capacity to pursue something difficult.

The objective is not merely to finish Dakar, but to earn the right to stand on the start line at 50 through disciplined preparation, structured capital allocation, and non-negotiable standards.

Discipline Compounds.

Performance does not expire with age. It compounds when experience, structure, and disciplined execution align.

This campaign operates under real-world constraints: executive responsibility, family leadership, financial governance, and capability development from zero.

Campaign Capital Structure

Foundation Sponsors (60–70%)

Primary capital engine covering bike acquisition, entry fees, logistics and core support.

Leadership Circle (10–20%)

Recurring monthly supporters via structured insights and campaign briefings.

Public Momentum (5–10%)

Short-term crowdfunding to demonstrate traction and community belief.

Projected Campaign Budget

A realistic privateer Dakar campaign requires disciplined financial planning.

  • Motorcycle acquisition & preparation
  • Dakar entry fees (~R385,000+)
  • Logistics & international shipping
  • Support crew & spares
  • Qualification rallies & training
  • Media production & leadership documentation

Estimated total campaign requirement: R3,000,000 – R5,000,000
