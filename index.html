The Mission

At 50 years old, I will stand on the start line of the Dakar Rally as a privateer. This is not impulsive ambition — it is a structured four-year performance campaign built on discipline, financial execution, skill development, and progressive qualification.

This journey combines executive leadership, endurance sport, and long-term strategic planning. Every kilometer is earned. Every milestone is intentional.

Campaign Roadmap

2026 – Foundation Year

• Learn and master off-road riding fundamentals
• Complete structured off-road training programs
• Enter beginner-level rally / navigation events
• Scale personal earnings to fund subsequent years
• Build physical conditioning base

2027 – Competitive Development

• Compete in South African cross-country events
• Advance roadbook navigation skills
• Develop multi-day endurance capability
• Formalize sponsor discussions
• Achieve 30–40% campaign funding target

2028 – International Exposure

• Enter African / international rally raids
• Simulate Dakar-style multi-stage events
• Refine professional bike setup
• Accelerate structured media output
• Reach 70% campaign funding target

2029 – Qualification & Execution

• Complete Dakar qualification requirements
• Final technical and navigation preparation
• Confirm logistics & service structure
• Secure full campaign funding
• Official Dakar entry submission

Why 50?

Because performance does not expire. Because leadership is demonstrated under pressure. Because resilience is built over decades.

This campaign represents long-term discipline — not midlife reaction. It demonstrates that structured execution, physical preparation, and strategic planning can redefine what is possible at 50.
