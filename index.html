A four-year strategic leadership campaign grounded in disciplined execution and gratitude — culminating in entry into the Dakar Rally at age 50.

Partner With This Campaign

Stewardship Over Ego

Journey to Dakar 2030 is grounded in gratitude — for health, family, provision, opportunity, and the capacity to pursue something difficult.

The objective is not merely to finish a race, but to earn the right to stand on the Dakar start line at 50 through disciplined preparation, capital responsibility, and non-negotiable standards.

Discipline Compounds.

Performance does not expire with age. It compounds when experience, structure, and discipline align.

This campaign operates under real-world constraints: executive responsibility, family leadership, financial accountability, and skill acquisition from zero.

Campaign Roadmap

2026 – Foundation

Off-road mastery, navigation training, financial scaling.

2027 – Development

South African cross-country events, endurance building.

2028 – Exposure

International rally participation, media acceleration.

2029 – Qualification

Dakar qualification completion and logistical execution.

2030 – Earn The Start Line

Gratitude. Readiness. Stewardship fulfilled.

Governance & Structure

This is not a hobby project. The campaign operates under executive governance principles:

  • Defined capital envelope (R3m–R5m projected)
  • Milestone-based funding strategy
  • Measured performance KPIs
  • Risk mitigation planning
  • Transparent reporting to partners
